## Personal Reflection

Most backend optimizations are not about writing “faster loops.”

They are about:

* avoiding repeated searching
* reducing memory usage
* minimizing unnecessary movement
* organizing data intelligently

DSA feels much easier when connected to real systems instead of memorizing patterns blindly.
